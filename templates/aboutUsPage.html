About CIRS

The Citizen Infrastructure Reporting System (CIRS) is designed to empower citizens to report infrastructure issues in their community efficiently. Our goal is to create a transparent and collaborative platform where users can highlight problems and track their resolutions.

With CIRS, you can easily report damaged roads, faulty streetlights, and other infrastructure concerns while staying updated on progress and resolutions.

Join us in making our communities better, safer, and more connected.
